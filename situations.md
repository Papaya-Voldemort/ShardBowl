## Game of Towers
  - Description: Competitors play a very real game of towers, consisting of leading a small army of 100 uninvested soldiers against each other.
  - Rules: All powers allowed.
  - Win Condition: The Last competitor with soldiers wins.
  - Notes: The focus of this event is leadership, but competitors may participate in combat as well.

## Battle of the Wits
  - Description: Competitors debate a high council of the chosen location on the matter of going to war against a nearby power (Country, city, planet, etc).
  - Rules: Competitors will have 5 minutes before the event to prepare for the debate; additionally, competitors are randomly assigned the views of Pro-War and Anti-War (No-War) by the council to ensure a good discussion.
  - Win Condition: The competitor that sways the council to vote for their assigned view is the winner.
  - Notes: Event ends after a 1-hour debate period, followed by a final vote of the council.
  - bullet:
    
## Strategic Team Combat
  - Description: A fun game of capture the flag! Participants are given 10 minutes to hide their flag, and then the game begins!
  - Rules: All powers allowed, no moving your flag after it's placed, fight to the death.
  - Win Condition: Get the other team's flag back to your base.
  - Notes: This is a fight to the death event, but upon death, the participants are sent to Braize (Hell Btw) and respawn after 8 minutes.
  - bullet:

## Contest of Champions
  - Description: A Contest of Champions, the ultimate battle! The competitor with the least investment picks the type of contest.
  - Rules: All contests must be very high stakes.
  - Win Condition: Determined by the type of contest.
  - Notes: If the amount of investiture is undeterminable, an unnamed shard will step in and select a fight to the death as the event.
